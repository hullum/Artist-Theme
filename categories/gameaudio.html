GAME AUDIO

I’ve worked as a freelance Sound Designer / Composer for various game projects during the last 8+ years. I also worked as the in-house audio guy at Kuuasema for several years.

I have good knowledge of audio design and implementation for games. I know my way around in Unity and I'm very familiar with doing audio scripting for games. I'm also familiar with the two most commonly used game audio middleware, Wwise and Fmod.
